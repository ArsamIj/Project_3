This dataset details the total number of electric vehicles (EVs) in Ontario. This includes both battery-electric vehicles (BEVs) and plug-in hybrid electric vehicles (PHEVs). Data includes: * Total EVs by Forward Sortation Area (FSA) (the first three characters of the postal code) of the vehicle’s registered address * Number of BEVs by FSA of the vehicle’s registered address * Number of PHEVs by FSA of the vehicle’s registered address
